About Us

Crafting Beautiful Spaces Since 2014

Welcome to Faisal Design, your trusted partner for premium interior design services in Lahore. With over 10 years of experience, we have transformed hundreds of homes and offices across Lahore and surrounding areas.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality interior design solutions at competitive prices. Today, we are proud to be one of the most recommended interior design companies in Lahore.

We specialize in wallpaper installation, PVC and wooden flooring, window blinds, curtains, carpets, and astro turf. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to delivering exceptional results.
